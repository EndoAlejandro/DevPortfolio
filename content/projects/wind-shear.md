Made for the **Cali Game Jam #2**, on the theme "Wind."

You play a cloud, the earth's last line of defense against a swarm of
biblically-accurate angels closing in to destroy it. An *Evangelion* inspired
premise built around one mechanic: you absorb air Kirby-style, but you can't
inhale enemies directly. Instead, you collect the orbs they drop on death,
and once you've absorbed enough, you unleash a blast that instakills anything
it touches.

It's a top-down survival loop: enemy spawn rate keeps climbing, the clock
keeps running down, and every fight is a decision about whether to keep
absorbing for a bigger payoff or cash in the blast now before you're
overrun.
