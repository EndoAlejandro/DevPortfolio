A top-down 3D roguelite where a lone gladiator fights waves of enemies with
only a sword and shield. The moveset follows a Hades-style rhythm move,
dash, attack, and defend/parry. Built around reading enemy tells and
punishing openings rather than button-mashing.

Built solo in one month for my second Master's degree project, this prototype
was a deliberate exercise in combat and AI systems programming:

- **Steering behaviours** driving enemy movement and positioning
- **Melee attack and parry systems**, including timing windows and hit
  feedback
- **Finite state machines** for both combat states and enemy behavior trees
- **Enemy AI** with multiple enemy variations, each reading and reacting to
  the player differently
- Getting proficient with **3D** after mostly 2D work up to this point

It's a prototype so it's still missing content and features but every system
above works end to end and was the point of building it.

Character design by [Muni](https://munitsuki.itch.io/). Remaining 3D assets
by [Kay Lousberg](https://www.kaylousberg.com/).
