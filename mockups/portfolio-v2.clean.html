About

Designer first, developer always.

I'm an interactive-media designer with a solid software-engineering background and 6 years of professional experience. For the last 3.5 I've specialised in Unity, mostly VR and mobile — and since 2021 I've poured my free time into side projects, honing gameplay dynamics and the elusive craft of game feel.

I recently graduated with honours from an MSc in Game Development at Universal Arts School, where I shipped several games as a solo developer. My goal is always the same: a final product that's captivating, satisfying, and genuinely enjoyable to play.
